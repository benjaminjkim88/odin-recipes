Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

So delicious and so good

Very tasty you should try

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bananas

Recipe Steps:

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice
  2. Spread Jelly on another slice
  3. Cut banana slices on one side of the bread
  4. Place two bread slices together and enjoy
