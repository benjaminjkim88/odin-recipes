Mac and Cheese

So delicious and so good

Very tasty you should try

Ingredients:

  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Milk
  • Macaroni
  • Croutons

Recipe Steps:

  1. Stir cheese and milk together in saucepan
  2. Once hot, add in macaroni
  3. Place macaroni in bake pan with croutons and extra cheese on top
  4. Bake at 450 F
