Ingredients:

  • 1.76 pounds rib eye
  • 1 onion
  • 2 stalks green onion
  • 1/2 carrot
  • 1 Tbsp toasted
  • 1 Tbsp toasted
  • 1 Tbsp cooking oil

Recipe Steps:

  1. Blend marinade ingredients
  2. Marinade beef with marinade
  3. Preheat Skillet and cook marinated beef
  4. Serve with rice
